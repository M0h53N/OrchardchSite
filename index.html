Introduction to Orchard Children Homes

Welcome to Orchard, we've been protecting and caring for our young people since 2017 across the East Midlands. We have been established after Orchard directors were inspired to provide opportunities to children in care.

At Orchard we believe that every child and young person deserves to be loved by dedicated, highly skilled adults, who work within safeguarding frameworks and go to great lengths to create a loving and diverse environment for young people.

Young people are our upmost priority, and our aim is to ensure that every young person is empowered to develop their social, emotional, independence and functional skills, enabling them to and take steps towards resilience, wellbeing, and educational opportunities.

Like an Orchard Garden we are devoted to nurture and support young people to flourish to their full potential.

Theraupetic Involvement

Orchard Children Homes offer clinical therapeutic input from a qualified psychologist Dr Coetzee.

Dr Coetzee is directly involved in training all staff in PACE (Playful, Acceptance, Curiosity, Empathy) and providing on-going clinical oversight to ensure staff practices are embedded amongst the homes.

Our Care Team have extensive knowledge in the following sectors:

Delayed Development
Child Sexual Exploitation
Non-Verbal Communication and Visual Aided Communication
Autism Spectrum Disorder
Hyper Attention Hyperactivity Disorder
Learning Difficulties
Substance Misuse
Self-Harm, Self-Injurious Behaviour and Suicidal thoughts
Attachment Disorder

We create a stable, nurturing environment where young people are supported to grow at their own pace
